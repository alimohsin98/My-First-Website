About Me



Hi my name is Ali, I have been collecting toys from a very young age. Recently I've collected all these minature toys I've found from kinder eggs that I am looking to sell. Please click on the Toys For Sale tab to see which toys are available to purchase.

Even though I love selling these minature toys. My dream and aspiration is to start a toy selling business. I would love to sell cooler and more high quality toys in order to make children and their families happy!

Therefore please purchase a toy to make a loved one happy and help me in the future.
